About Us

What is Networthy

Networthy is a platform designed to help individuals relocate to new cities with ease, providing trustworthy information and support through verified local guides called 'CityMates'.

Our Story

We all come from different parts of Karnataka and united in Bangalore. During a hackathon at NIT Jalandhar, we faced the challenge of traveling alone to Punjab without knowing anyone there. This experience highlighted the fear of relocating to an unfamiliar place. We realized the need for a trusted guide in new cities to help us focus on our goals. Unable to find such a solution online, we decided to create Networthy.

The Present

As part of amBITion'24, we presented this idea and were selected to build on it. Here is our solution in front of you.

In Future

We want to remove the fear of relocating with our solution so that if we get any opportunities such as internships, jobs, or anything else, we should never reject that opportunity just because we need to travel or we don't know anyone in the new city.
