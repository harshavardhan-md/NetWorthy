Challenges Faced
by Students and Working Professionals

Moving to a new city can be daunting, especially for students and working professionals. Finding trustworthy information and local support can be challenging and stressful.

We bring you the Solution

Our platform connects you with CityMates who guide you and help you understand your new city. Here's how it works:

  1. Fill the Form: Provide your name, email, and the city you're relocating to.
  2. Get Assigned a CityMate: Based on your preferences such as profession, age, and interests, we match you with a suitable CityMate.
  3. Start Your Journey: Your CityMate will guide you and provide valuable insights about the new city.
