About Us

Networthy is a decentralized platform connecting individuals moving to new cities with verified local guides, "CityMates." These guides provide personalized support, helping users find accommodations, safe neighborhoods, and essential services for a smooth, stress-free relocation.



Our mission is to eliminate the anxiety of relocating by providing reliable local assistance. Networthy connects users with trusted CityMates for insights and guidance, ensuring a smooth transition.
